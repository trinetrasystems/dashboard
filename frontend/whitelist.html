Badge Whitelist
Whitelisted badges are exempt from loitering alerts — their cards are auto-muted in the banner, excluded from the active alert count, and still appear normally in the detection log.
auto-mute behavior
When a badge is added here, any active loitering alert for that badge is immediately muted — the alert card greys out in the banner and the active alert count drops. The badge still appears in the Currently in Premises table with a WHITELIST tag and all sightings remain in the detection log.
